How to Use

For Customers

1. Register: Create a new account using the Registration page.

2. Browse: Login and click "View Cars" to view our cars.

3. Search: Use the search bar to find SUVs, Sedans, Available cars, etc.

4. Book: Click "Rent Now" on an available vehicle and select your dates.

5. History: View your reservations on your Dashboard.

For Administrators

Login: Use your Employee ID (1 for admin) and password.

User List: View all registered customers in the system.

Transactions: View a master list of all rentals, dates, and total costs.
